Task Runner

So what exactly is a Task Runner?

A task runner is a node plugin that automates a bunch of heavy responsabilities for you.

Task Runners provide developers the ability to spend the moajority of their time on coding, rather than trying get everything working together.

  • Central location for all connections to be made
    • The task runner will run your server, and boot up your app for you.
  • What else can a task runner do?
    • A task runner can inject all your script files into your index.html for you.
    • A task runner can jshint all your javascript, conact all your files together, and minify your concated file
    • It can also do the same thing for css, or sass, or less, or even stylus, also other javascript preprocessors like CoffeeScript
    • A task runner can watch and reload all your files on save
    • A task runner can handle a lot of other things as well
  • There are currently two popular and widely used task runners
  • Gulp
    • Most recommended, because it is easiest to use
    • Gulp also uses less CPU on your computer.
  • Grunt
    • Very difficult to use.
    • I don't reccomend using it, unless you feel like you like the syntax more than gulp.
